About Us

At Penguin Wood Craft, we are passionate about creating unique, handcrafted wooden products inspired by the beauty of the natural world. Our journey began in the Pacific Northwest, where we discovered a love for the outdoors and a desire to bring that beauty into our customers' homes.

We are committed to using sustainable materials and practices in every aspect of our business. Our products are lovingly crafted using foraged, reclaimed, and restored materials from the Pacific Northwest and Oregon Coast. Each piece reflects the tranquility of nature and is designed to bring a touch of the outdoors into your home.

In addition to our commitment to the environment, we also take pride in our craftsmanship and the quality of our products. Our range of handcrafted wooden creations includes home decor, functional art, and customized pieces tailored to your individual style and preferences.

Thank you for visiting Penguin Wood Craft. We are excited to share our passion for handcrafted wood products with you and hope that our creations bring joy and inspiration to your home.
